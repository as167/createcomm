About CreateComm

CreateComm is a place where talented artists from around the world can interact with commissioners. It is not always easy for artists to get their art noticed, so we built this community in order for undiscovered artists to get discovered. Commisioners who want custom art that fit their style can come to CreateComm and send a request, artrist can then accept the request and have a private chat with the commisissioner.
